## Product Signal

This repo captures an earlier iteration of my portfolio as a product surface: a place to test narrative, hierarchy, project framing, and first impressions. The goal was to think about a portfolio the way a PM thinks about any user-facing experience: who is it for, what should they notice first, and what action should it drive?

## What I Explored

- Personal positioning and project hierarchy
- Visual design direction for a technical/product portfolio
- Clearer storytelling around projects, experience, and signal
- User-journey thinking for recruiters, collaborators, and hiring teams

## Why It Matters

A portfolio is more than a static site. It is a product experience around credibility, clarity, and momentum. This repo reflects iteration on that experience.
