Portfolio Toprani is a portfolio iteration focused on visual direction, project storytelling, and recruiter-facing product narrative.

It treats a personal portfolio as a product surface: a place to test hierarchy, first impressions, audience intent, and the story a hiring team should understand within seconds.

**Links:** [Source repository](https://github.com/dhruvtoprani/Portfolio-Toprani)

## Product Snapshot

| Area | Signal |
| --- | --- |

## Product Signal

This repo captures an earlier iteration of my portfolio as a product experience. It reflects the same thinking used in PM work: who is the user, what do they need to understand first, what should they trust, and what action should the surface drive?

## What I Explored

- Personal positioning and project hierarchy
- Visual design direction for a technical/product portfolio
- Clearer storytelling around projects, experience, and signal
- User-journey thinking for recruiters, collaborators, and hiring teams

## Why It Matters

A portfolio is more than a static site. It is a product experience around credibility, clarity, and momentum.
